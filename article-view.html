Augmented Reality (AR) glasses have finally hit the mainstream. The new VisionWrap X1 offers distinct advantages over its predecessors, with a battery life that lasts all day and a form factor indistinguishable from regular sunglasses.

Key Features

Forget asking for the weather. The new wave of home hubs, led by the Nexus Home Pro, can manage your entire digital life. It proactively suggests schedule adjustments, optimizes home energy usage, and even orders groceries before you run out.

Automation is moving from reactive to predictive. These devices learn your habits with scary accuracy, promising to save the average user up to 10 hours a week in menial tasks.
