Business Guidelines

Essential guidelines and policies for businesses using CompareHubPrices. Learn about our requirements, best practices, and how to maximize your business presence on our platform.

  1. Home
  2. Business Account

Introduction

Welcome to CompareHubPrices Business Guidelines. These guidelines are designed to help businesses create a successful presence on our platform while maintaining high standards of service and customer satisfaction.

By using CompareHubPrices as a business, you agree to follow these guidelines. Failure to comply may result in account restrictions or removal from the platform.

Key Requirements

Accurate Product Information

All product listings must contain accurate, up-to-date information including prices, specifications, and availability. Misleading or false information is strictly prohibited.

Competitive Pricing

Maintain competitive pricing that reflects current market conditions. Prices should be updated regularly to ensure accuracy.

Reliable Shipping & Delivery

Provide clear shipping information, delivery times, and tracking details. Ensure products are delivered as promised.

Customer Support

Respond to customer inquiries promptly (within 24 hours) and provide excellent customer service throughout the purchase process.

Quality Standards

Only list products that meet quality standards. Ensure all products are genuine, properly described, and in good condition.

Best Practices

  • Regular Updates: Keep your product listings, prices, and inventory updated regularly to maintain accuracy.
  • High-Quality Images: Use clear, high-resolution product images from multiple angles to help customers make informed decisions.
  • Detailed Descriptions: Provide comprehensive product descriptions including all relevant specifications, features, and benefits.
  • Transparent Policies: Clearly communicate your return, refund, and warranty policies to customers.
  • Professional Communication: Maintain professional and courteous communication with customers at all times.
  • Timely Responses: Respond to customer inquiries, reviews, and feedback promptly and professionally.
  • Inventory Management: Keep accurate inventory counts and mark items as out of stock when unavailable.

Prohibited Practices

The following practices are strictly prohibited and may result in immediate account suspension or termination:

  • Listing fake, counterfeit, or fraudulent products
  • Providing false or misleading product information
  • Price manipulation or bait-and-switch tactics
  • Spam, unsolicited communications, or harassment
  • Violation of intellectual property rights
  • Failure to deliver products as described
  • Unprofessional or abusive behavior towards customers
  • Circumventing platform policies or fees

Account Management

Maintain your business account with accurate information:

  • Keep business contact information up to date
  • Verify your business credentials regularly
  • Monitor your account for notifications and updates
  • Review and respond to customer feedback
  • Update business hours and availability
