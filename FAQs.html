General Questions

What is CompareHub?

CompareHub is a price comparison platform that helps users find the best deals on electronics and tech products across South Africa. We aggregate prices from various retailers and present them in an easy-to-compare format, helping you make informed purchasing decisions.

Our service is completely free to use, and we don't sell products directly. Instead, we connect you with the retailers offering the best prices on the products you're interested in.

Is CompareHub free to use?

Yes, CompareHub is completely free for all users. There are no subscription fees or hidden costs for using our price comparison service.

We are able to offer our service for free because we may earn a commission from retailers when you click through our site and make a purchase. This doesn't affect the price you pay, and it helps us continue to provide and improve our comparison service.

Which retailers do you compare?

We compare prices from major South African electronics and tech retailers. Our platform is currently being developed to include partnerships with various retailers.

Coming soon - we are working on integrating with multiple retailers to provide you with the most comprehensive price comparison possible.

Stay tuned for updates as we expand our retailer network to bring you the best deals from across South Africa.

Do you sell products directly?

No, CompareHub does not sell products directly. We are a price comparison service that helps you find the best deals across different retailers.

When you find a product you want to purchase, we'll direct you to the retailer's website where you can complete your purchase directly with them. We don't process any transactions or handle any payments ourselves.

Account Questions

Do I need to create an account to use CompareHub?

No, you don't need an account to browse products and compare prices on CompareHub. Our basic price comparison functionality is available to all visitors.

However, creating a free account gives you access to additional features such as:

  • Saving products to your wishlist
  • Setting price alerts for specific products
  • Viewing your browsing history
  • Receiving personalized recommendations

Creating an account only takes a minute and enhances your experience with our service.

How do I create an account?

Creating an account on CompareHub is quick and easy:

  1. Click on the "Sign In" button in the top right corner of any page
  2. Select "Sign up here" at the bottom of the login form
  3. Enter your first name, last name, email address, and create a password
  4. Accept our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
  5. Click "Create Account"

You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account. Click the link in the email, and you're all set!

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it by following these steps:

  1. Click on the "Sign In" button in the top right corner
  2. Select "Forgot Password?" below the login form
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account
  4. Click "Send Reset Code"
  5. Check your email for a password reset link
  6. Follow the link to create a new password

If you don't receive the reset email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or try again.

Price Comparison Questions

How often are prices updated?

We update our prices multiple times daily to ensure you're seeing the most current information. Most product prices are refreshed at least once every 24 hours, with popular products being updated even more frequently.

However, please note that prices can change rapidly at the retailers' websites. While we strive for accuracy, we always recommend confirming the final price on the retailer's website before making a purchase decision.

Each product listing shows a "Last Updated" timestamp so you can see when the price was last checked.

How do you compare prices?

Our price comparison system works by:

  • Collecting product data and prices from various retailers through automated systems
  • Matching identical products across different retailers
  • Standardizing the information to make comparison easy
  • Displaying the results in an easy-to-understand format

We take into account the base price of the product as well as any current promotions or discounts being offered by the retailer. We also provide information about delivery costs where available, so you can compare the total cost of purchasing from different retailers.

Our goal is to provide a comprehensive and transparent comparison that helps you find the best overall deal.

Can I set price alerts?

Yes, registered users can set price alerts for specific products. When you set a price alert, we'll notify you by email when the price drops below your specified threshold.

To set a price alert:

  1. Find the product you're interested in
  2. Click on the "Set Price Alert" button on the product page
  3. Enter your target price
  4. Click "Save Alert"

You can manage all your price alerts from your account dashboard.

Why do prices sometimes differ from the retailer's website?

While we strive to provide the most accurate and up-to-date pricing information, there are several reasons why you might occasionally see differences:

  • Prices change frequently, and there might be a short delay in our updates
  • Some retailers have dynamic pricing that changes based on various factors
  • Special promotions or flash sales might start or end between our update cycles
  • Regional pricing differences might apply in some cases

We always recommend confirming the final price on the retailer's website before making a purchase decision. If you notice a significant price discrepancy, please let us know so we can investigate and update our system.

Wishlist Questions

How do I add products to my wishlist?

To add a product to your wishlist:

  1. Find the product you want to save
  2. Click on the heart icon next to the product
  3. The product will be instantly added to your wishlist

You need to be logged in to use the wishlist feature. If you're not logged in, you'll be prompted to sign in or create an account when you click the heart icon.

How do I view my wishlist?

You can view your wishlist in two ways:

  • Click on the heart icon in the top navigation bar to open a quick view of your wishlist
  • Go to your account dashboard and select the "Wishlist" tab for a full view with more options

From your wishlist, you can:

  • See current prices across all retailers
  • Track price changes over time
  • Set price alerts
  • Remove items from your wishlist
  • Move items directly to a retailer's site for purchase
Is there a limit to how many products I can add to my wishlist?

No, there is no limit to the number of products you can add to your wishlist. You can save as many products as you like for future reference or price tracking.

However, for better organization, we recommend periodically reviewing your wishlist and removing items you're no longer interested in.

Technical Questions

Which browsers are supported?

CompareHub is designed to work with all modern web browsers. For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

While older browsers may still work, you might experience limited functionality or display issues. We recommend keeping your browser updated to ensure the best performance and security.

Is CompareHub mobile-friendly?

Yes, CompareHub is fully responsive and works well on all devices, including:

  • Desktop computers
  • Laptops
  • Tablets
  • Smartphones

Our website automatically adjusts to provide the best experience based on your screen size. All features are available on mobile devices, allowing you to compare prices on the go.

What should I do if I encounter a technical issue?

If you encounter any technical issues while using CompareHub, please try these steps first:

  1. Refresh the page
  2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  3. Try using a different browser
  4. Check your internet connection

If the issue persists, please contact our support team with the following information:

  • A description of the issue
  • The device and browser you're using
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots if possible

You can reach our support team at support@comparehub.co.za.
